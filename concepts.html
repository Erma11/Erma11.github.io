String Theory

Somes referred to as the 'Theory of Everything'. It tries to explain how gravity and quantum physics fit together. The theory states that all objejcts in our universe are composed of vibrating filaments and membranes.

Space-Time

Combines Space nad Time into a single idea. This helps to comprehend how the universe works on larger and smaller levels.

Nanotechnology

Manipulation of matter on a molecular scale. Constructs items form the bottom up.

Antimatter

Material composed of antiparticles. Scientists believe that every particle has an identical antimatter counterpart with an opposite charge. When a particle meets its antiparticle, it disappears in a burse of light.

Expanding Universe

The universe has no center. The galaxies are moving away from us in space, yet space is also moving. Edwin Hubble was the first to prove that the universe is expanding. This is called 'Hubble's Law'.

Entanglement

Phenomenon which occurs when two or more objects have to be described with reference to each other, even when they are spatially separated.
